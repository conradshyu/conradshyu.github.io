Shuffle Match

Synopsis

Shuffle Match is a novel sliding block game. The game is a mixture of sliding blocks logic and Tetris. The objective is to shuffle squares of the same color, red, green, yellow, and blue, to form a line. The line will then disappear. The colors of squares are randomly generated. The game continues until the new line is filled to the top of the game matrix.

Click Mew Game to start a new game.
The objective is to shuffle the squres of different colors, yellow, blue, red, and green to match the layout on the left.
